Core Skills What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning? That I have the ability to become a programmer, I have learnt about my own weaknesses and how to overcome them, and that I can find it difficult to ask for help when I need it. What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming? Values role in programming is essentially what that person values in their coding. For example, some people may code for efficiency, or code for meeting a deadline, or code for readability. All that changes between these people are their values which shows the role they play in programming. Empathy and self awareness are important for when you are giving and receiving feedback to do with programming. If you are not empathetic with someone while giving feedback you may come across too harsh but if you do not have enough self awareness you may disregard any feedback you get even if it is good feedback for you to improve on. What has surprised you the most about the core learning? The most surprising part about core learning was how helpful it was to reflect inwards and especially how useful headspace was with setting a proper routine and just practising mindfulness. What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning? The most challenging aspect was definitely the introspection. It is always a difficult task to look inwards and really think. Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course? Because programmers are people too, and if you start teaching them from the core about just how to be functional people then all other areas of their life will benefit, this includes but isn’t limited to programming. Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer. It doesn’t feel like a waste of time. I think this because it has helped to teach me about myself more and allowed a deeper understanding of how to combat issues that can come up in general. This can help in many ways such as preparing for entering the industry and general communication with people as well as helping you identify and fix any weaknesses and strengthen any strengths you find. What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp? Re-evaluate your strengths and limitations from Sprint 2. Has your idea of these changed over the course of Foundations? Have you discovered any new ones during this course? How do you think these will help/hinder you during Bootcamp? One of my biggest strengths coming into bootcamp will be my ability to pick things up quickly, with a some background knowledge of programming. A limitation will be concentrating for long periods of time. I tend to not be able to concentrate for long without needing to walk around and readjust.
